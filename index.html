Namaste!

Educate but don’t classify people.
有教無類。

— Confucius (孔子)

Learning and teaching is a way to share life. To use a term from Thich Nhat Hanh, it is interbeing.

In the Chinese tradition, being a teacher is like being a parent in that you dedicate yourself to help others develop their potential. It is a labor of love and the joy comes from witnessing their blossoming.

Life is about doing things and giving them our best efforts because what we do, and what we don’t do, affects others.
