Permaculture Workshop Report

Permaculture is an eco-design science that uses deep observation and applies methods and techniques into eco-design work to mimic natural systems and adapt them to our education, economy, housing, farming and way of living. Free for Butte College students, faculty and staff. Donation suggested for community members.

Attend one of the three workshops and write a 350-400 word report on what you learn from it, you can earn up to 30 extra-credit points. (The report needs to be posted by 11:59 p.m. Saturday, 9/30. Click the above link and then click on to post your report.) Click here for workshop info.
