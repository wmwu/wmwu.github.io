Why People Are So Upset About Wall Street’s ‘Fearless Girl’

The “Fearless Girl” statue, a bronze sculpture of a young girl with her hands on her hips, standing defiantly in front of Wall Street’s Charging Bull has certainly made an impression. The sculpture is currently set to remain on display until 2018, but critics are calling for its immediate removal.
