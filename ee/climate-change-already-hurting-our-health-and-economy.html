Climate Change Already Hurting Our Health and Economy, Report Warns

Climate change is already killing people, hurting their health, worsening air pollution and helping the spread of infectious diseases. Environmental disruptions are also affecting economies and costing hundreds of billions of dollars a year, mostly because of extreme weather events.
