Why Life Does Not Really Exist

Even today, scientists have no satisfactory or universally accepted definition of life. ... No one has ever managed to compile a set of physical properties that unites all living things and excludes everything we label inanimate. ... In trying to define life, we have drawn a line at an arbitrary level of complexity and declared that everything above that border is alive and everything below it is not.
