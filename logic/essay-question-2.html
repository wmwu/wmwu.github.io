Prejudice in Fancy Dress

  1. Why does Whyte object to using mystery as an excuse to believe whatever one wants to believe?
  2. What are Whyte's reasons for contending that faith without evidence is mere prejudice?
  3. Why does Whyte think that Pascal's Wager is not a good reason for believing in Christianity?

Sum up Whyte's reasons for his viewpoints without commenting on whether you agree or disagree with his views.
