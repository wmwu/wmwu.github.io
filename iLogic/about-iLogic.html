about iLogic

iLogic is a free online interactive textbook for introductory college level logic courses. In addition to doing a small part to make higher education more affordable and accessible, the textbook is developed with the main goal of helping students improve their learning.

The Butte College Professional Advancement Project allowed me to devote more time to this project. It was completed and made available here in August 2008. Special thanks go to Kenneth Meier and Illeana Gantt for their encouragement, and to Ty Benoit and Jim Schmidt for the computer hardware and software used in this project. I would also like to thank my family for their understanding and support. My son Kevin’s help in programming should be mentioned as well.

Revisions and updates were made in summer 2010.
